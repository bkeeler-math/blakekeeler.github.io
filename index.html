About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mathematics at UNC Chapel Hill. My doctoral advisor is Professor Yaiza Canzani, and my research interests include partial differential equations, microlocal and semiclassical analysis, scattering theory, and spectral geometry.
